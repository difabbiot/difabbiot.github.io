May 2026 Project Milestone

LINERFUN officially kicks off — Month 1 of 36

The Marie Skłodowska-Curie Global Fellowship LINERFUN (Grant 101206371) has officially started at Politecnico di Torino. This first phase (M1–6) is dedicated to project setup: data management plan, risk management plan, career development plan, and the dissemination & exploitation plan — all being drafted and submitted to the REA.

The incoming phase will run through October 2026 at POLITO (Turin), before I move to the University of Melbourne for the outgoing phase (M7–30). In parallel, the literature review on piTDIBC methods is ongoing and the CINECA LEONARDO HPC proposal is being prepared.

2026 Publication

New paper accepted in AIAA Journal

The paper "Enhancing one-equation turbulence model for delta wing flows by gene expression programming" (Kotlzowski, Di Fabbio, Tangermann, Klein, Fang, Sandberg) has been accepted for publication in the AIAA Journal. The work demonstrates how interpretable symbolic corrections derived via GEP can extend Spalart–Allmaras predictions to complex multi-delta vortical flow configurations.

2026 Conference

32nd AIAA/CEAS Aeroacoustics Conference

Co-author on the paper "GPU-Accelerated Large-Eddy Simulations of High-Speed Cavity Flows With Dynamic Doors Deployment" (Bres, Ivey, Heidt, Wang, Bose, Hottois, Guerrero, Di Fabbio), presented at the 32nd AIAA/CEAS Aeroacoustics Conference, pp. 3359.

2025 Conference

20th OpenFOAM Workshop — Vienna

Two contributions at OFW20 in Vienna: "Towards a generalized open-source turbulence model for external aerodynamics" and "Verification and Validation of OpenFOAM for High-Speed Aerodynamics", both co-authored with Segalerba, Rossano, and Guerrero (Leonardo Innovation Labs).

2025 Award

MSCA Global Fellowship awarded — Seal of Excellence

The proposal LINERFUN was awarded a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Global Postdoctoral Fellowship under the Horizon Europe programme (Call HORIZON-MSCA-2024-PF-01). Total funding: €309,153.72. The project also received the Seal of Excellence, recognising its scientific quality. Project start: May 2026.

Aug 2024 Career

PhD defended Summa cum Laude

Defended the doctoral thesis "Efficient Turbulence Modelling for Vortical Flows from Swept Leading Edges" at the Universität der Bundeswehr München with final grade Summa cum Laude.
