Overview

Research Areas

Aeroacoustics & Acoustic Liners

Time-domain impedance boundary conditions, duct acoustics, liner optimisation for UHBR nacelles. Current focus of the LINERFUN project.

Scale-Resolving CFD/CAA

LES, SAS, and hybrid RANS/LES for high-fidelity aeroacoustic simulations. HPC deployment on CINECA LEONARDO, NCI Australia, POLITO LEGION.

Delta-Wing Vortical Flows

Vortex formation, breakdown, shock–vortex interactions. RANS failure analysis and corrections for high-agility military aircraft configurations.

Physics-Informed ML & GEP

Gene Expression Programming for turbulence model augmentation. Solver-in-the-loop training for deployable, interpretable closures.

High-Speed Compressible Flows

Supersonic/hypersonic aerodynamics, cavity flows with dynamic doors, digital twin frameworks for aerospace systems.

Rocket Propulsion CFD

High-fidelity simulation of LOX/CH₄ sub-scale rocket combustors. Combustion stability and thermal analysis for propulsion system design.

Journal Articles

  • D. Kotlzowski, T. Di Fabbio, E. Tangermann, M. Klein, Y. Fang, R. D. Sandberg
    Enhancing one-equation turbulence model for delta wing flows by gene expression programming
    AIAA Journal, 2026

    Designed symbolic GEP corrections introducing nonlinear stress–strain terms and a revised turbulence production term; trained on a single delta wing and validated on double and triple delta configurations, enabling an interpretable and robust modelling workflow.

    AIAA Journal · 2026
  • T. Di Fabbio, E. Tangermann, M. Klein, Y. Fang, R. D. Sandberg
    Strategies for Enhancing One-Equation Turbulence Model Predictions Using Gene-Expression Programming
    Fluids, 2024, 9(8)

    Developed two GEP pathways extending the Boussinesq assumption and constructing a new one-equation closure using solver-in-the-loop training for deployable wall-bounded turbulence models.

    Fluids · 2024
  • K. Rajkumar, T. Di Fabbio, E. Tangermann, M. Klein
    Physical aspects of vortex–shock dynamics in delta wing configurations
    Physics of Fluids, 2024

    Quantified shock–vortex interaction mechanisms across double and triple delta planforms through CFD and enstrophy transport analysis, identifying geometry- and incidence-driven buffet sensitivity.

    Physics of Fluids · 2024
  • T. Di Fabbio, K. Rajkumar, E. Tangermann, M. Klein
    Towards the understanding of vortex breakdown for improved RANS turbulence modelling
    Aerospace Science and Technology, 2024

    Diagnosed physical and modelling causes of one-equation RANS failures in transonic sideslip and proposed a validated modification improving stability and accuracy.

    Aerospace Science and Technology · 2024
  • T. Di Fabbio, E. Tangermann, M. Klein
    Analysis of the vortex-dominated flow field over a delta wing at transonic speed
    The Aeronautical Journal, 2023

    Produced high-fidelity datasets resolving vortex systems and shocks; analysed eddy viscosity and Reynolds stresses in vortex formation and maintenance.

    The Aeronautical Journal · 2023
  • T. Di Fabbio, E. Tangermann, M. Klein
    Investigation of transonic aerodynamics on a triple-delta wing in sideslip conditions
    CEAS Aeronautical Journal, 2022

    Demonstrated RANS limitations and advantages of high-fidelity approaches for predicting vortex and shock interactions, improving aerodynamic load predictions.

    CEAS Aeronautical Journal · 2022
