📌 pinned MSCA May 2026

LINERFUN — my Marie Skłodowska-Curie project

I am an MSCA Global Fellow working on acoustic liners for UHBR aero-engines. LINERFUN (Grant 101206371) runs for 36 months across Politecnico di Torino, University of Melbourne, and ONERA Toulouse. The core challenge: physics-informed Time-Domain Impedance Boundary Conditions (piTDIBC) for scale-resolving CFD/CAA.
